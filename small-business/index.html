Use Cases for Small Business

New Employee Onboarding

When a new hire starts, pull up available inventory and assign their equipment in seconds. Generate a checklist of everything they're receiving, capture their signature digitally, and have a complete record from day one.

Employee Offboarding

When someone leaves, instantly see all equipment assigned to them. Generate a return checklist, track what's been returned, and flag any missing items—before their last day. Protect your company from equipment loss.

Budget Planning

See which devices are aging out and need replacement. Track warranty expirations and repair costs to forecast your IT budget accurately. Know exactly when that 3-year-old MacBook fleet will need refreshing.

Insurance & Compliance

Generate a complete inventory report for insurance purposes. In case of theft or disaster, you'll have serial numbers, purchase dates, and values documented. Also useful for SOC 2 and other compliance requirements.
