Developer Use Cases

MDM Integration

Sync device data between ArbInq and your MDM solution (Jamf, Intune, Mosyle). When a device enrolls in MDM, automatically create it in ArbInq. Keep serial numbers, OS versions, and compliance status in sync.

HR System Automation

Connect to Workday, BambooHR, or your HRIS. When a new employee is created, trigger a workflow to assign available equipment. When they're terminated, automatically flag their devices for collection.

Help Desk Integration

Link ArbInq to Zendesk, ServiceNow, or Jira. When a support ticket mentions a device, automatically pull up its history, warranty status, and assigned user. Create repair tickets directly from the API.

Custom Reporting Dashboards

Pull inventory data into your BI tools. Build custom dashboards in Tableau, Grafana, or your own application. Query device counts, utilization metrics, and cost data through the API.
